Track 04 — "Slow Country" arrangement notes

Working session log. Decisions, doubts, and what to try next time the studio is unlocked.

§The shape of it

Tempo settled at 78. The bridge has been the sticking point — the demo version pushes to 86 and it kills the patience the song is trying to earn. Slowing the bridge feels right but the cellos drop out of pocket. Re-record with a click but ask Maren to play through the click rather than locked to it.

Decision: keep tempo flat at 78 across the whole track. The variation should come from the arrangement, not the BPM. (Confirmed in studio Mon evening.)

§Open questions

  • Get a tempo reference Maren is comfortable with
  • Decide if pedal steel is in or out of the chorus
  • Re-record cello bed for bridge with new tempo
  • Try the verse with no kick drum — see if it earns its return
  • Lyric pass on verse 3 — "porchlight" again, do better
